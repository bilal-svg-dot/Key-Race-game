HOW TO PLAY

1. The Challenge

Words will appear on the screen. Your goal is to type them as quickly and accurately as possible.

2. Scoring System

  • +10 points for each correct word
  • Extra time added for correct words
  • Higher difficulty gives more points

3. Difficulty Levels

Easy

30 seconds start time
+5 seconds per word

Medium

20 seconds start time
+3 seconds per word

Hard

15 seconds start time
+2 seconds per word

4. Statistics

Track your performance with:

  • WPM: Words per minute
  • Accuracy: Percentage of correct words
  • Streak: Consecutive correct words
  • High Score: Your best performance

5. Tips for Success

Focus on the screen, not the keyboard

Stay calm under time pressure

Practice regularly to improve

Aim for accuracy first, then speed
